About Us:

In this club, We are trying to Break Down the unnecessary Boundaries between different [Academic Disciplines], between [School and Industry], between [Employment and Entrepreneurship], and between [Countries, Languages, Cultures] ..., and between [PEOPLE], to make collaborations more Accessible.

-

BUT, we don't know how to make this happen.
All we can do is to keep testing new Hypothesis.

-

Fortunately, we do know where the solution is: It lies in you!
Yes, everyone of [YOU]

Join us, explore and grow with us.

"Loners Club" -- Yes, you are a loner, but you are not alone!

Constantly have a lot of things must-to-do, but sometimes don't want to do any of them. I know what I have to do, but still keep inventing excuses not doing it.

Come and see someone who feels the same in your campus. We talk a little bit, re-assure ourselves "Yes I must and Yes I can!", and go through our to-do things together. It's a silent mutual spiritual support to each other. It requires courage to admit being week. Feel free to say it here.

Graduate school can be tough, depressing and overwhelming. but you are definitely not alone.

Need a mechanism to bring people together with minimal communication cost.
